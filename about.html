Qhum Qhaks

Vumbuka means ‘get up’ in zulu. Vumbuka Records was created on a spirit of being open minded towards unfamiliar sounds and cultures and therefore the label is not limited to only one kind of music. The first release from Vumbuka Records is Qhum Qhaks by Sandy B. The release comes in the form of a vinyl containing seven tracks and a download code for the album + three bonus tracks. The release party for Qhum Qhaks will take place at Dizonord in Paris on May 18. For promotional purposes Sandy B will also visit Paris-based online radio, Le Mellotron, Kiosk Radio in Brussels and Rush Hour in Amsterdam.
